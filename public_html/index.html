Services

GST Registration

Documents Required

1.Pan card.
2. AADHAR card.
3. Passport size Photo.
4. Recent Electricity Bill for place of business and PAN card of owner(Pan card of electricity bill holder)
5. Mobile number registered with aadhar.
6. Name of Business
7. Nature of Business

Limited Liability Partnership Formation

Documents Required

1) KYC of partners:- Pan card, Aadhar card.
2) Latest Bank statement of partners.
3) Voting card Or Driving license.
4) Electricity bill for place of business.
5) Email id and mobile number of partners.
6) passport size photos of partners.
7) Name and nature of Business.

Import export code (IEC)

Documents Required

1) Gst certificate or shop establishment.
2) Current account cancel cheque.
3) Aadhaar card.
4) Pan card.
5) Email id.
6) Aadhar link mobile no.
7) Electricity Bill.
8) Rent agreement or consent letter

INCORPORATION OF PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY

Documents Required

1). Director Documents (Min 2 Director):- *VPan Card.*Aadhar Card.*Driving Licence.*Voter ID.*Passport.*Latest Two Months Bank Statements. *Mobile No*Email id
2).Business Place Documents. *Latest Electricity Bill.*Rent Agreement or NOC
3).Name and nature of Business.
4).If Any Relative Property:- *Latest Electricity Bill.*Concent Letter.

DIGITAL SIGNATURE DOCUMENT.(DSC)

Documents Required

1) Adhar card.
2) pan Card.
3) Passport size Photo.
4) Email id.
5) Mobile no.
